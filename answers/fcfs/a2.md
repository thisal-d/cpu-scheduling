### **Solution to FCFS Practice Question 2**

**Gantt Chart:**
`| P1 (0-5) | P2 (5-8) | P3 (8-15) |`

**Waiting Times:**
* P1: 0 ms
* P2: 5 ms
* P3: 8 ms

**Turnaround Times:**
* P1: 5 ms
* P2: 8 ms
* P3: 15 ms

**Averages:**
* Waiting time = $(0+5+8)/3 = 4.33$ ms
* Turnaround time = $(5+8+15)/3 = 9.33$ ms
