### **Solution to Question 4: Round Robin (RR)**

**Gantt Chart:**
Time Quantum ($q$) = 4 ms. Processes arrive at $t=0$.
Order: $P_1, P_2, P_3$.
*   $P_1$ runs 0-4 (Remaining: 8).
*   $P_2$ runs 4-8 (Remaining: 2).
*   $P_3$ runs 8-12 (Remaining: 0). **$P_3$ Finishes.**
*   $P_1$ runs 12-16 (Remaining: 4).
*   $P_2$ runs 16-18 (Remaining: 0). **$P_2$ Finishes.** (Only needed 2ms, finishes early).
*   $P_1$ runs 18-22 (Remaining: 0). **$P_1$ Finishes.**

`| P1 (0-4) | P2 (4-8) | P3 (8-12) | P1 (12-16) | P2 (16-18) | P1 (18-22) |`

**Calculations:**

1.  **Turnaround Time (TAT) = Completion Time - Arrival Time:**
    *   $P_3$: Completes at 12. TAT = $12 - 0 = 12$ ms.
    *   $P_2$: Completes at 18. TAT = $18 - 0 = 18$ ms.
    *   $P_1$: Completes at 22. TAT = $22 - 0 = 22$ ms.

2.  **Waiting Time (WT) = TAT - Burst Time:**
    *   $P_3$: $12 - 4 = 8$ ms.
    *   $P_2$: $18 - 6 = 12$ ms.
    *   $P_1$: $22 - 12 = 10$ ms.

**Filled Table:**

| Process | Burst Time (ms) | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{1}$ | 12 | 10 | 22 |
| $P_{2}$ | 6 | 12 | 18 |
| $P_{3}$ | 4 | 8 | 12 |
