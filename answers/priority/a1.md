### **Solution to Question 3: Priority Scheduling**

**Gantt Chart:**
The processes are scheduled based on priority (Smallest Number = Highest Priority).
Order: $P_2 (1) \to P_4 (2) \to P_1 (3) \to P_3 (4)$.

`| P2 (0-1) | P4 (1-6) | P1 (6-16) | P3 (16-18) |`

**Calculations:**

1.  **Waiting Time (WT):**
    *   $P_2$: Starts at 0. WT = $0$ ms.
    *   $P_4$: Starts after $P_2$ ($0+1$). WT = $1$ ms.
    *   $P_1$: Starts after $P_4$ ($1+5$). WT = $6$ ms.
    *   $P_3$: Starts after $P_1$ ($6+10$). WT = $16$ ms.

2.  **Turnaround Time (TAT) = WT + Burst Time:**
    *   $P_2$: $0 + 1 = 1$ ms.
    *   $P_4$: $1 + 5 = 6$ ms.
    *   $P_1$: $6 + 10 = 16$ ms.
    *   $P_3$: $16 + 2 = 18$ ms.

**Filled Table:**

| Process | Burst Time (ms) | Priority | Waiting Time (ms) | Turnaround Time (ms) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| $P_{2}$ | 1 | 1 | 0 | 1 |
| $P_{4}$ | 5 | 2 | 1 | 6 |
| $P_{1}$ | 10 | 3 | 6 | 16 |
| $P_{3}$ | 2 | 4 | 16 | 18 |
| **Average** | **-** | **-** | **5.75 ms** | **10.25 ms** |
