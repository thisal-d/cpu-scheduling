### **Solution to Question 5: Preemptive SJF (SRTF)**

**Step-by-Step Execution Logic:**
1.  **Time 0:** $P_1$ arrives. Only process available. Runs.
    *   Remaining $P_1$: 7 ms.
2.  **Time 1:** $P_2$ arrives (Burst 4).
    *   Compare $P_1$ (Rem 7) vs $P_2$ (Rem 4). $4 < 7$.
    *   **Preempt $P_1$**. Start $P_2$.
3.  **Time 2:** $P_3$ arrives (Burst 9).
    *   Compare $P_2$ (Rem 3) vs $P_3$ (Rem 9). $3 < 9$. Continue $P_2$.
4.  **Time 3:** $P_4$ arrives (Burst 5).
    *   Compare $P_2$ (Rem 2) vs $P_4$ (Rem 5). $2 < 5$. Continue $P_2$.
5.  **Time 5:** $P_2$ finishes (Started at 1, ran for 4ms).
    *   Remaining processes: $P_1(7)$, $P_3(9)$, $P_4(5)$.
    *   Shortest is $P_4(5)$. Start $P_4$.
6.  **Time 10:** $P_4$ finishes (Started at 5, ran for 5ms).
    *   Remaining: $P_1(7)$, $P_3(9)$.
    *   Shortest is $P_1(7)$. Resume $P_1$.
7.  **Time 17:** $P_1$ finishes (Started at 10, ran for 7ms).
    *   Remaining: $P_3(9)$. Start $P_3$.
8.  **Time 26:** $P_3$ finishes.

**Gantt Chart:**
`| P1 (0-1) | P2 (1-5) | P4 (5-10) | P1 (10-17) | P3 (17-26) |`

**Calculations:**

1.  **Completion Times (CT):**
    *   $P_2$: 5
    *   $P_4$: 10
    *   $P_1$: 17
    *   $P_3$: 26

2.  **Turnaround Time (TAT) = CT - Arrival Time:**
    *   $P_1$: $17 - 0 = 17$ ms.
    *   $P_2$: $5 - 1 = 4$ ms.
    *   $P_3$: $26 - 2 = 24$ ms.
    *   $P_4$: $10 - 3 = 7$ ms.

3.  **Waiting Time (WT) = TAT - Burst Time:**
    *   $P_1$: $17 - 8 = 9$ ms.
    *   $P_2$: $4 - 4 = 0$ ms.
    *   $P_3$: $24 - 9 = 15$ ms.
    *   $P_4$: $7 - 5 = 2$ ms.
